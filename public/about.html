About KisanTrack 🌾

Transforming agriculture with transparency and trust.

🌍 Context

Agricultural products go through multiple stages such as farming, harvesting, transportation, storage, and selling through intermediaries. Due to limited transparency, farmers and buyers often lack visibility into the crop’s journey. This leads to inefficiencies, quality issues, and unfair pricing.

⚡ Challenge

The challenge is to create a system that can track crops across the entire supply chain — including farmers, transporters, warehouses, intermediaries, and sellers — while maintaining reliable records of crop origin, movement, and quality.

🎯 Goal

KisanTrack aims to develop a digital platform that enables end-to-end crop traceability. It allows stakeholders to monitor produce from cultivation to final sale, improving transparency, efficiency, and trust in the agricultural supply chain.
