How It Works

Seven steps.
Complete traceability.

Every crop batch gets a unique ID and travels through a verified, timestamped digital journey visible to all stakeholders.

Step 01
🌾
Farmer Harvest
Farmer registers the batch with crop type, weight, and farm location. A unique QR batch ID is generated instantly on any mobile device.
→
Step 02
🔬
Quality Check
Certified agents record moisture, grade (A/B/C), and pesticide clearance before the crop moves forward in the chain.
→
Step 03
🏭
Processing Hub
Cleaned, sorted, and packed at a primary processing facility. Weight and condition are re-verified and digitally updated.
→
Step 04
🚛
Live Transport
Vehicle, temperature, and ETA are tracked live. Automatic alerts fire if temperature thresholds are crossed during transit.
Step 05
🏪
Warehouse Storage
Cold chain facilities log daily temperature, intake weight, and planned dispatch date — all linked to the batch ID.
→
Step 06
🤝
Intermediary Sale
Trade agents log the agreed price, making margins transparent to farmers and protecting them from exploitation.
→
Step 07
🛒
Final Market Sale
The buyer receives a verified digital certificate showing the crop's full journey — origin, quality, and every hand it passed through.
Result
📊
Full Transparency
Every rupee, every kilometre, every temperature reading — on one live dashboard for every stakeholder.
Platform Features

Everything your supply
chain needs.

Built for farmers, transporters, warehouse managers, and buyers — one platform, every stakeholder connected in real time.

🗺️
End-to-End Traceability
Every batch has a timestamped journey from farm gate to final sale. Scan any QR code to see the full verified history instantly.
🌡️
Temperature Alerts
Automated alerts fire when storage or transit temperature crosses safe thresholds — preventing spoilage before it happens.
📊
Price Transparency
See the full price journey — farm gate to consumer — so farmers know their fair share and buyers can trust the supply chain.
🔬
Quality Management
Digital quality certificates, grade records, and pesticide test results — all linked permanently to the batch and farmer ID.
🏪
Warehouse Integration
Cold chain facilities log intake, daily temperature checks, and dispatch — inventory is always real-time and fully auditable.
📱
QR Scan and Update
Field agents scan batch QR codes on any mobile device to update stages, add photos, and log handoff signatures — no app needed.
